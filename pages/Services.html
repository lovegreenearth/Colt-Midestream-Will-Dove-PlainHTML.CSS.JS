What We Do

Colt Midstream is a producer-focused natural gas gathering and processing company in the Fort Worth Basin and Barnett Shale. Colt is privately-owned and operated. Headquartered in Houston, TX, Colt also has field offices in Weatherford, Springtown, Eastland, Jacksboro, and Decatur. We consistently serve our producers through superior customer service and reliability.

Colt owns and operates over 3700 miles of pipeline, over 100,000HP of compression, and 135 MMscfd of active processing capacity across 14 Texas counties

Springtown Plant

The Springtown Gas Plant is Colt’s largest facility with over 20,000HP of onsite compression, 80MMscfd of gas processing capacity and 6000bpd of condensate stabilization capacity.

Weatherford Plant

The Weatherford Gas Plant is Colt’s most advanced facility with over 17,000HP of onsite compression and 75MMscfd of processing capacity serving the eastern region of our system.

Lone Camp Plant

The idled Lone Camp plant has a processing capacity of 40MMscfd. Portions of the facility remain in active service providing supplemental compression to Colt’s gathering system.

Gordon Plant

The 40MMscfd Gordon Gas Plant serves as the primary processing plant for the western region of Colt’s system.

Jacksboro Compressor Station

The Jacksboro Compressor Station was initially constructed in the 1950s during the emergence of the Ft. Worth Basin. Updated continuously over the years, it now stands as Colt’s largest compressor station with over 10,000 available horsepower.

Huckabay Plant

Colt’s Huckabay Plant has a 20MMscfd capacity and is utilized as a swing plant to provide backup processing capacity for both unscheduled outages and regularly scheduled maintenance at our other facilities.

Natural gas gathering and processing

Colt’s system gathers and compresses raw natural gas from wells and transports it to gas processing plants through low-pressure pipelines. Once the gas arrives at our processing plants, it’s processed into natural gas liquids (NGLs) and dry residue gas.

Transmission of NGL and residue gas to market

Once natural gas liquids (NGLs) and residue gas emerge from a plant, they’re ready for distribution to energy markets. Colt’s pipelines take the products from the processings plants to end markets, which distribute gas to the community for heating and powering homes.

Condensate processing (stabilization)

Unrefined (high vapor pressure condensate and off-spec NGL) gas product needs additional processing to become a marketable energy source. We take this commodity and process it into on-spec NGL product and a high-gravity crude stream.
